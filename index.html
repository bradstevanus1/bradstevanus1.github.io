Latch

Latch is a 2D mechanics-based bullet-hell game very similar to Realm of the Mad God. You control a player who has multiple forms of movement (walking, grappling, dashing) who must fight through dynamics levels containing unique mobs with different attack and movement techniques. Keep your skills sharp, this won't be easy. Click this to learn more.
